## Dolphin

![Dolphin](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0e/Bottlenose_dolphin_with_young.JPG/1599px-Bottlenose_dolphin_with_young.JPG)

Dolphins are aquatic mammals within the infraorder Cetacea. Dolphins are closely related to whales and porpoises. They are found worldwide, mostly in shallow seas of the continental shelves, and are carnivores, mostly eating fish and squid.
